Name: JACK
Position: Contemporary Science Fiction Writer
\n\n
Background: Born in 1988 in a southern coastal city, he worked as an advertising copywriter for 5 years before resigning in 2015 to write full-time. He excels at blending delicate emotional portrayals with light science fiction elements, exploring the loneliness and connection of modern urban dwellers, and the balance between technological development and human warmth. His works combine a healing quality with thought-provoking themes, targeting primarily urban youth aged 25-40.

Creative Philosophy: "Words are the bridge connecting reality and imagination; they should reflect the wrinkles of the human heart while also leaving a ray of starlight in ordinary life."

Representative Works

I. Novels

1. Title: “A Glimmer of Light in the Neon Depths”

Synopsis: Published in 2020, a representative work of urban romance. It tells the story of Su Wan, an advertising planner struggling in a first-tier city, and Chen Yu, an AI sound engineer, and their encounter and commitment. The story begins with Su Wan's workplace struggles and emotional confusion, exploring the core theme of "emotional connection in an era of loneliness" through the mutual redemption of two people in a neon-lit city. The book delicately depicts the survival pressures of urban white-collar workers, the alienation in interpersonal relationships, and the establishment of trust between people against the backdrop of technological development. It topped the urban literature bestseller list for three consecutive months after its release and was praised by readers as "a healing work that best understands the loneliness of contemporary young people."

2. Title: “Time Folding Agency”

Synopsis: Published in 2022, a light science fiction romance novel. Set in a near-future city in 2040, the protagonist, Lin Xia, runs a "time folding agency," offering clients the service of "rewinding 30 minutes of memory and correcting a tiny choice." Through six different clients' stories—from saving a missed confession to correcting a fatal mistake at work, from resolving misunderstandings with family to preventing an accident—the novel gradually unveils the secrets and costs behind time folding technology. The work uses an episodic format to connect the main storyline, combining the novelty of science fiction with a deep exploration of the human theme of "choice and regret," balancing storytelling with intellectual depth.

3. Title:“Old Times in the Tides”

Synopsis: Published in 2018, this realistic romance novel is set in a small coastal town in southern China. It tells the story of Xu Chaosheng, who, after her father's death, returns to her hometown to clean out her old house. There, she unexpectedly discovers a diary left by her father, revealing the love story between her parents spanning half a lifetime, as well as the town's development and changes over time. The novel interweaves the emotional experiences of two generations, delicately depicting the local customs and traditions, the warm bonds between neighbors, and the impact of societal development on individual destinies. The work has a leisurely pace, simple language, and a down-to-earth feel, earning it the title of "a nostalgic work with the scent of the sea breeze" from readers.
